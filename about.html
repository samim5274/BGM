About Us

Inspired By Excellence & Innovation

Founded on 19th September 2021, BGM IT began with a powerful mission: empowering individuals—especially the educated but unemployed—to be equipped with the tools, training, and attitude they need in order to thrive in the digital economy.

We believe that technology should not only be useful for a select few, but should become accessible to all. Whether you are a university graduate looking for what's next, or a would-be professional looking to pursue the freelancing way, BGMI IT is here to show you the path.

Our institution is not just a training institute. We are building an end-to-end IT company where learning, growth, and job take place in tandem. Our double-pronged strategy allows us to serve students as well as clients—giving rise to a proprietary ecosystem where students acquire experience by solving actual problems.

Our Vision

We envision a world in which no one is left behind due to lack of an opportunity. Anyone educated by BGMIIT should be able to dictate their own fate globally and locally. We areexpanding, with an ambition to be aworld-class IT company that offers education, software solutions, and client services worldwide. We are building an international community of self-assured, skilled professionals who are technology-driven change agents.

Our Mission

To break the cycle of unemployment by spreading IT knowledge and digital skills to every corner of society. We are dedicated to helping individuals—regardless of background—build careers based on talent and ability. By creating an inclusive learning environment, we aim to guide students toward self-reliance through freelancing, remote jobs, or full-time work.
